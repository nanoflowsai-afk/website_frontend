Website Theme Guidelines

Brand Colors
- Primary: #4F46E5 (indigo)
- Secondary: #22C55E (emerald)
- Accent: #F59E0B (amber)
- Neutral 900/700/500/300/100: #0F172A / #1E293B / #64748B / #CBD5E1 / #F8FAFC
- Surface: #0B1021 (hero), #111827 (panels), #FFFFFF (cards on light)

Typography
- Headings: Inter, weight 700/800, tight tracking
- Body: Inter, weight 400/500
- Code: JetBrains Mono or monospace fallback
- Heading scale: H1 44/52, H2 32/40, H3 24/32, body 16/24

Layout
- Max content width: 1200px, gutters 24px
- Grid: 12 columns on desktop, 6 on tablet, 4 on mobile
- Section spacing: 64px desktop, 48px tablet, 32px mobile
- Card padding: 20–24px desktop, 16px mobile
- Border radius: 16px cards, 9999px pills, 10px inputs/buttons

Hero Section
- Background: primary-to-surface gradient or abstract glow
- Layout: left text stack, right visual (illustration or stats)
- CTA buttons: primary solid + secondary outline with hover lift

Navigation
- Sticky top bar with translucent backdrop blur
- Active link color: primary; hover underline offset 4px

Buttons
- Primary: indigo solid, white text, shadow subtle
- Secondary: outline neutral-300 with hover fill to neutral-100
- Tertiary: text-only with underline on hover

Forms
- Inputs: neutral-100 background, neutral-300 border, radius 10px
- Focus ring: 2px primary, offset 2px; inline error text in #EF4444

Cards and Panels
- Elevation: subtle shadow (0 8px 24px / 12%) on dark surface
- Use accent badge in top-right for status or tags

Lists and Tables
- Stripe rows with neutral-900 / neutral-800 on dark
- Headers uppercase, letter-spacing 0.04em, weight 600

Icons and Illustration
- Line icons at 20–24px; use accent sparingly for highlights
- Imagery style: abstract gradients, glassmorphism accents

Interactions
- Hover: translateY(-2px) and 4% brighter background
- Focus: visible ring; avoid relying on color alone
- Motion: 150–220ms ease for small UI, 350ms for modals

Breakpoints (px)
- Mobile: 0–639
- Tablet: 640–1023
- Desktop: 1024–1439
- Wide: 1440+

Dark/Light
- Dark default; light variant swaps surfaces to white and neutral text to #0F172A
- Ensure 4.5:1 contrast on body text; 3:1 on UI controls

Data Viz
- Use indigo, emerald, amber, sky (#0EA5E9), rose (#F43F5E) palette
- Gridlines light neutral; tooltips glassy with subtle shadow
